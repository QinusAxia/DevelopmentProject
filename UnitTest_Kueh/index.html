Instructions

1. Press spacebar or mouse click to elevate plane

2. Aim is to avoid hitting the bottom, top and barriers for as long as possible

3. Click "Faster" button to speedup the game

4. Click "Slower" button to slowdown the game

5. Each time you hit the obstacle, health reduces. You start with 200 HP

6. When your health hits 1, and you hit an obstacle the plane crashes and gameover

HAVE FUN
